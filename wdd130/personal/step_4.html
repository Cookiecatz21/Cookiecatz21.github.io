Step 4: Background

A backstory and characteristics makes a character unique. Start by picking a background based on who your character was or is. Your background will give you a few extra proficiencies and traits. Each background has permade permade personality traits, ideals, bonds and flaws to get you thinking about who your character is. if youll be using these pick two personality traits and one each for the other three. Also think what your character looks like.

Example backgrounds:

The Soldier background in D&D represents a character with a history of military service. These individuals have honed their combat skills and experienced the discipline, camaraderie, and trials of army life.

The Sage background in D&D represents a character who has dedicated their life to the pursuit of knowledge. These individuals are scholars, researchers, or experts in various fields of study.

The Criminal background in D&D is for characters with a history of breaking the law.
